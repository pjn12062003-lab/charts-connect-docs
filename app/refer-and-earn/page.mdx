---
title: Refer and Earn
---

The **Refer and Earn** program allows you to invite others to join **Charts Connect** and earn commission every time they subscribe to a paid plan.

Your referral network can extend up to **three levels** , allowing you to earn from both direct and indirect referrals.

### **How It Works**

Each user on Charts Connect gets a **unique referral link** that can be shared with others. When a new user registers through your link, they automatically become part of your referral network.

You will earn commission whenever users under you make a paid subscription. It is the same for all plans, whether monthly, bi-annual, or annual.

### **Registration via Referral Link**

*   You share your unique **referral signup link** (it includes your sponsor code).
*   When a new user opens your link, the **referral code** will be automatically filled in on the **registration form** .
*   Once the new user completes signup, they are registered **under your referral code** , linking them to your referral network.

Example Referral Link: chartsconnect.com/auth/signup?referral=CCR-abcd1234

### **Affiliate Page**

All referral-related data and tools are available on the **Affiliate page** under **Refer and Earn** .

Here you can view:

#### **Referrer Information**

*   The details of your own Referrer (if you registered under someone).
*   This includes the Referrer’s name.

#### **Your Referral Code**

*   A unique code assigned to you for inviting new users.

#### **Your Referral Signup Link**

*   A ready-to-share link containing your referral code.
*   Anyone registering using this link will automatically appear under your account.

#### **Referral Tree View**

*   Displays the **network of users** under your account in a hierarchical (tree) structure.
*   Shows all **direct** and **indirect** referrals (up to 3 levels).
*   Each user node displays:
*   Username
*   Referral level (1st, 2nd, 3rd level)
*   **Commission earned** from that user

#### **Commission Wallet Total**

*   Displays the **total commission amount** earned from all referrals combined.

This total is updated automatically when commissions are generated after successful subscription payments.

### **Commission Listing**

**Refer and Earn → Commission Listing**

This page shows a **detailed breakdown** of all commissions earned from your referral network.

Each entry includes:

*   Referred user name
*   Subscription details
*   Date of payment
*   Level (1st, 2nd, or 3rd)
*   Commission amount credited to your wallet

This helps you track all your referral income over time.

![](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/documentation/1762093803235-64627403-eda2-48cd-9509-83b8c0b79505.png)

### **Commission Flow and Levels**

Commissions are automatically distributed, whenever a referred user makes a subscription payment through the **Stripe payment system** .

The distribution is as follows:

![](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/documentation/1762094372480-9cc16609-851c-483e-9ed4-3f663db1ee03.png)

**When it triggers:**

*   Every time a referred user’s subscription payment succeeds on Stripe
*   Applies to **monthly** , **bi-annual** , and **annual** plans
*   Automatically calculated and added to your **commission wallet**

### **Example Flow**

Let’s say:

*   You invite **User A** → You earn **15%** on their payments (Level 1).
*   **User A** invites **User B** → You earn **2.5%** on User B’s payments (Level 2).
*   **User B** invites **User C** → You earn **2.5%** on User C’s payments (Level 3).

Each time these users renew their subscriptions, your commission is automatically credited.

### **Tips**

*   Share your referral link with traders, communities, or friends interested in automated trading.
*   The more active users you refer to use Charts Connect, the higher your recurring earnings.
*   Commissions are real-time and automatically handled by the payment system.
*   Your referral link never expires.
