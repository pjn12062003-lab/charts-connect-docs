---
title: Send To Platforms – Forex
---

The **Send To Platforms** feature allows you to forward your trading signals from **Charts Connect** directly to supported trading platforms.

For Forex traders, Charts Connect provides a dedicated **Expert Advisor (EA)** that connects with **MetaTrader** using your **Forex Token**.

### **Step 1: Generate Your Forex Token on Charts Connect**

*   Log in to your **Charts Connect** account.
*   Go to the **Send To Platforms** section.
*   Open the **Forex** page.
*   Add a name and click on **“Create EA Token”** to create your unique **Forex Token**.
*   Select the token as Send To in the master token page
*   Copy the generated token — you will use it inside MetaTrader to link your account.

Each Forex Token is unique to your user account and identifies your trading connection with MetaTrader.

### **Step 2: Charts Connect Expert Advisor (EA)**

*   Open your **MetaTrader** application.
*   In the **Navigator** panel, find **Expert Advisors**.
*   Find the **Charts Connect EA** in your Expert Advisors list.

### **Step 3: Add the EA to Your Chart**

*   Drag and drop the **Charts Connect EA** onto the chart you wish to automate.
*   When prompted, open the **Inputs** tab (or **Common** settings, depending on your MetaTrader version).
*   Fill in the following fields:

**Token****:** Paste your **Forex Token** (from Charts Connect)

**Username****:** Enter your **Charts Connect registered email address**

**URL****:** https://chartsconnect.com

*   Click **OK** to save and activate the EA.

### **Step 4: Confirm the Connection**

Once setup is complete:

*   The EA will automatically connect to your **Charts Connect account** using your token and email.
*   You’ll see a confirmation message or indicator on your chart showing **Connected**
*   Any valid signals received from Charts Connect will now execute in your MetaTrader account automatically.

### **Manage or Revoke Access**

You can manage your Forex tokens anytime from your **Charts Connect Forex page**:

*   **View existing tokens**
*   **Regenerate** or **delete** tokens
*   **Disable** MetaTrader access instantly for security or turn off Algo Trading if needed.

## **Forex Token Add Alternative**

#### **Add Forex Tokens via Master Token Page**

In addition to creating Forex tokens directly from the **Forex page**, you can also create and manage them through the **Master Token** page.

This method is especially useful when you’re already managing your main trading tokens and want to link them to your **MetaTrader Expert Advisor (EA)** quickly.

### **Step 1: Go to the Master Token Page**

*   Log in to your **Charts Connect** account.
*   Navigate to the **Master Token** section from your dashboard.
*   You will see a list of all your existing **Master Tokens** (used for TradingView and other integrations).

### **Step 2: Click + “Send To” Against a Master Token**

Each Master Token entry has a **Send To** column.

*   Locate the token you want to connect with Forex.
*   Click the + under **Send To** column.
*   A popup will open showing available platform options.

### **Step 3: Select “Forex”**

In the popup, choose **Forex** as the platform you want to connect this Master Token to.

This action links your TradingView signals (or other sources using this token) with your MetaTrader Forex connection.

### **Step 4: Choose or Create a Forex Token**

After selecting **Forex**, a new popup will appear listing your existing **Forex Tokens**.

You’ll see:

*   A list of all previously created Forex tokens
*   A **Create EA** button to generate a new token if needed

**Options available:**

*   **Select an existing Forex Token** to use it for this Master Token.
*   **Click “Create EA”** to generate a new token for your Forex connection.
*   The new token will automatically appear in your list once created.
*   You can then assign it to your Master Token directly from this popup.

### **Step 5: Confirm and Link**

Once you’ve selected or created a Forex token:

*   Click **OK** in the popup.
*   The selected Forex Token will now be linked to the chosen Master Token.
