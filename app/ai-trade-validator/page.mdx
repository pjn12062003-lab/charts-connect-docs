## **AI Trade Validator**

Our **AI Signal Validation** Model is a state-of-the-art feature built into your **Charts Connect Expert Advisor** that acts as your personal AI trading analyst. Before your EA enters any trade, you have the option to let our AI model validate each signal and calculate a probability score for success between 0% and 100%.

To enable this option, you need a **paid plan.** Simply toggle the AI validation feature on from the Auto AI Validation toggle in our EA and set your minimum confidence threshold from the Minimum AI Score to Execute Alert input and let the AI filter out low-probability trades automatically.

### **How It Works**

*   Turn on the AI validation feature in your Expert Advisor settings.
*   Choose your minimum probability requirement (e.g., 70%, 80%, 90%)
*   The higher the threshold, the more selective your EA becomes
*   Lower thresholds allow more trades but with varied confidence
*   Find your sweet spot based on your risk tolerance
*   Let AI Validate Every Trade
*   Signal is automatically sent to our AI validation system
*   AI analyzes the trade in real-time
*   Trade Only High-Confidence Signals
*   Probability above your threshold: Trade enters automatically.
*   Probability below your threshold: Trade is skipped.
