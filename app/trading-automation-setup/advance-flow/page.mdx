# Advanced Trading Automation Setup

This guide is designed for experienced users who want to integrate their own TradingView alerts or custom webhook signals with Charts Connect.

> [!IMPORTANT]
>
> **Prerequisites**
>
> Before starting this guide, ensure you have:
> - An active Charts Connect account
> - MetaTrader 5 installed on your computer
> - A TradingView account with alert capabilities
> - Basic understanding of trading automation concepts

## Overview

Charts Connect is a trading automation platform that connects signal sources (such as TradingView) to trading targets (MetaTrader accounts). The New Automation wizard walks you through 5 steps to get live.

<Steps>

### Choose Automation Mode

Click **+ Create New Automation** in the sidebar to open the wizard. On Step 1 of 5, choose one of two modes:

![Create New Automation](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773138003908-f3814b91-1a97-4bb5-84a3-cb415ea359c0.jpeg)

- **Simple Mode** - Pick a pre-built strategy from the Charts Connect library. Recommended for beginners.
- **Advanced Mode** - Use your own TradingView alerts or custom webhook signals. For experienced users.

![Advanced Mode](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773138135775-c390acfe-bf09-4beb-b0d3-55e221308a6b.jpeg)

For this guide, select **Advanced Mode**.

**Add Token (Advanced Flow)**

After selecting Advanced Mode, the **Advance Flow: Add Token** dialog appears.

![Token** dialog box](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773214129537-99bce072-f8f1-4f48-8984-72a728b5ed2b.jpeg)

1. **Friendly Name** - Enter a label to identify this automation (e.g., `EURUSD RSI`, `GoldBot`, `My Custom Strategy`)
2. Click **Add Token** button to create the token and proceed to Step 2

### Select Sources

Available source options:

- ✅ **TradingView** - Receive alerts from TradingView webhooks (Recommended)

1. **Name This Account** - Enter a label for this MT account (e.g., `Demo EA Flow`, `Live Account 1`)

![Demo EA Flow](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773214545457-dabcfb32-d807-4fde-8f08-2e4183a0e013.jpeg)

2. Click **Create EA Token** - Generates a new connection token for this account

![Search by Account Name](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773214710388-62f4c602-47c3-486f-93d9-411059e1cd28.jpeg)

3. **Search by Account Name** - Filter existing saved accounts by name (if you have multiple accounts)

![Search by Account Name](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773230661569-ebdda4d8-c73f-440a-90af-323b53fb0dca.jpeg)


### Install Expert Advisor (EA) for MT5

**One-time MetaTrader 5 setup to enable automated trading**

Step 4 is a two-part process (Sub-step 1 and Sub-step 2) shown via a numbered indicator at the top of the dialog. This step installs the Charts Connect bridge into your MetaTrader 5 terminal.

**Sub-Step 4.1 — MetaTrader 5 Setup Guide:**

This guide will walk you through setting up the Charts Connect Expert Advisor (EA) on your MetaTrader 5 platform.

1. **Download the EA File** - Click **'Download EA (v2.1.0)'** to download the Charts Connect package to your computer

2. **Access the Experts Folder**
   - In the upper-left corner of your MetaTrader 5 platform, click **File → Open Data Folder**

    ![Open Data Folder](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773053764318-416466e1-a44a-47bd-886c-76a64b63d61a.png)

   - In the Data Folder that opens, click into the **MQL5** folder
   - Inside the MQL5 folder, click into the **Experts** folder

    ![Expert folder](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773053951709-7dfcb45f-0912-4d59-970b-29e2ce52bab7.png)

3. **Add Charts Connect Files to the Experts Folder**
   - In your Experts folder, extract the contents of the .zip file you downloaded in Step 1

    ![Experts folder location](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773054129122-270d8753-ef6a-4a75-bb76-58f6d4c9a03a.png)

   - Ensure the following files are placed directly into the Experts folder:

     ![ChartsConnect DLL](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773054643712-54525d70-ecf9-4230-9b8c-124ef89ca2b4.png)

4. **Allow Algorithmic Trading and DLL Imports**
   - At the top of your MetaTrader 5 platform, click **Tools → Options**

    ![Tools option](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773054807810-b5ca6913-1e5f-421d-8381-5b9e9c5af4a5.png)

   - Go to the **Expert Advisors** tab

    ![Expert Advisors tab](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773054904535-3d9d9cb7-52ab-4ac0-a2c7-eaf7c32f41cd.png)

   - Tick **'Allow Algorithmic Trading'** and **'Allow DLL imports'**
   - Click **OK**

5. **Enable Algo Trading**
   - In the MetaTrader 5 toolbar (at the top of the terminal), locate the **Algo Trading** button

    ![Algo Trading button](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773054995120-9cd071e7-f79e-492b-93e5-cc3e8da5d9d2.png)

   - If the button is red, click it to enable algorithmic trading
   - The button should turn green, indicating it's active

6. **Attach Charts Connect EA to a Chart**
   - Open the Navigator panel in MetaTrader 5 by pressing **Ctrl+N** (or by clicking **View → Navigator**)

    ![Navigator](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773055175087-743655ea-b509-42cb-aedf-60aba50b9cf9.jpeg)

   - In the Navigator panel, right-click on **Expert Advisors**
   - Click **Refresh**
   - Locate the **Charts Connect MT5 EA** in the list, right-click on it, and select **'Attach to Chart'**
   - Choose any chart (e.g., XAUUSD M15)

   ![Attach to Chart](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773055364091-b78ff623-d999-4afc-a1b0-acaf2efeef48.png)

> [!NOTE]
>
> **Do I need to attach the Charts Connect EA to every chart if I want to trade multiple symbols?**
>
> Yes, Charts Connect works on a per-symbol basis. Each instance of the EA monitors and executes trades only for the symbol of the chart to which it's attached.
>
> **Example Setup:**
> - Attach Charts Connect EA to the EURUSD chart → trades EURUSD signals
> - Attach Charts Connect EA to the GBPUSD chart → trades GBPUSD signals
> - Attach Charts Connect EA to the XAUUSD chart → trades XAUUSD signals
>
> Each EA instance maintains its own connection, dashboard, and alert queue for its specific symbol. You can use any timeframe for the chart - the timeframe doesn't affect signal reception, only the symbol matters.

7. **Configure EA Settings**
   - A properties window for the EA will appear
   - Under the **Common** tab, ensure **Allow Algo Trading** is ticked

    ![Algo tarding ticked](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773055662630-abd0826f-98e5-41b0-b7ac-a27bc863db56.png)

   - Under the **Dependencies** tab, ensure **Allow DLL imports** is ticked

    ![DLL ticked](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773055752835-3e0a01eb-1fd3-4a40-8983-475df9db2b83.png)


8. **Input Your Token Key and Configuration**
   - Open the **Inputs** tab in the EA properties window
   - Enter your **Username/Email** in the Username field
   - Paste your **Token** into the Token field

    ![Token Configuration](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773055993060-cef51c58-2c67-4dbd-8126-92faf9cb5fee.png)

   - Click **OK** - The EA will now attempt to establish a connection

> [!TIP]
>
> **Can I use my Charts Connect license on different trading accounts?**
>
> Yes, absolutely! Charts Connect provides flexible licensing. This means you're free to connect to any trading account you choose – whether it's Demo or Live, or even a Prop account. Just ensure you're not exceeding your license's concurrent connection limit.

9. **Verify Connection Status**
   - Navigate to the **Toolbox** panel at the bottom of MT5 (press **Ctrl+T** if hidden)
   - Click on the **Experts** tab
   - Look for connection confirmation messages from Charts Connect EA

    ![connection confirmation messages](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1773056163524-a1d7e954-5ec3-4196-86d9-5344b88d7265.png)

   - You should see messages like:
     - "Charts Connect EA initialized successfully"
     - "WebSocket connection established"
     - "Connected to Charts Connect server"

10. **Understanding the Dashboard**
    - Once connected, the Charts Connect EA will display a modern dashboard on your chart showing:
      - **Connection Status:** Real-time WebSocket connection indicator
      - **AI Validation Scores:** Probability scores for incoming signals (if enabled)
      - **Alert Queue:** Pending signals awaiting review or execution
      - **Execution Controls:** Manual execution buttons for queued alerts

![Confirm Installation](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1772864966895-321fc888-50c9-4fc0-aa7a-8c4406f6b4b6.jpg)

11. Click **'I've Installed It'** - Confirm installation and proceed to Sub-step 4.2

> [!NOTE]
>
> **All done with the MT5 setup!**
>
> Next, let's try sending a few test signals to confirm your setup is running smoothly. Check out our Testing Your Connection guide to learn how to send test alerts from TradingView or other sources.

**Troubleshooting EA Connection Issues:**
> [!WARNING]
>
>If your EA is not connecting:
>- Verify your Email/Token is correct
>- Check that Algo Trading is enabled (green button in toolbar)
>- Ensure DLL imports are allowed in Options
>- Check the Experts tab for error messages
>- Verify your firewall isn't blocking the connection

**Sub-Step 4.2 — Configure Your Connection Token:**

After confirming EA installation, you will be shown your unique Connection Token in a highlighted yellow box:

![Connection Token](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1772865199374-ad56d6b2-1532-42a6-93e4-2b41bb09b10e.jpg)

Copy this token and paste it into the EA settings when you add it to your chart.

1. **Copy the token** - Click the copy icon next to the token string, or manually select and copy it
2. **Open MetaTrader 5** - Switch to your MT5 terminal
3. **Open EA Properties** - Double-click the EA on the chart, or right-click → Properties
4. **Paste the token** - In the EA Settings/Inputs tab, paste the token into the designated field
5. **Enable AutoTrading** - Click OK and ensure the AutoTrading button in MT5 toolbar is green/active
6. Click **'I've Done This Step'** - Confirms the token has been applied 

**EA Setup Confirmation Screen:**

![EA Setup Confirmation](https://media.chartsconnect.com/uploads/howitworks/1772865615084-1c4d4c73-2a82-49ba-8be6-655e15abb1d4.jpg)

After completing both sub-steps, the dialog shows a green confirmation banner

Your strategy is now communicating with your MT5 terminal.

## Troubleshooting

> [!WARNING]
>
> **If Connection Does Not Establish**
>
> If your EA doesn't connect after a few minutes, try these steps:
>
> 1. **Verify AutoTrading is ON** - Check the AutoTrading button in MT5 is highlighted/green
> 2. **Confirm EA is on chart** - The EA must be dragged onto the correct chart (XAUUSD M15)
> 3. **Verify token is correct** - Re-check that the pasted token matches exactly (no extra spaces)
> 4. **Check internet connection** - Both your computer and Charts Connect need active internet
> 5. **Click 'Troubleshoot Setup'** - Use the troubleshoot button in the dialog for guided diagnostics
> 6. **Click 'Open Dashboard'** - Returns to the main Dashboard to monitor connection status
